## Lessons Learned

- Quota management for Batch & Matrix is significantly different from a typical sync API — on-callers must be familiar with it.
- Impact assessment (health check analysis) is a key skill for on-callers.
- Region turn-off and component rollout restart pipelines are the primary immediate action tools.
- Integrations between Batch/Matrix and [Apigee](https://cloud.google.com/apigee)/[Gloo](https://www.solo.io/products/gloo-gateway/) are important to be aware of, even if not always the primary concern.
